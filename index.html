Welcome !

Satellite allows to visualize a TLP data from a quite large number of TLP setups: Oryx, Barth, HANWA, HPPI, LAAS, SERMA. Basically, it allows to compare TLP IVs, visualize a TLP IV curve with its leakage evolution, to select and visualize transient current and voltage associated to a TLP point and visualize the leakage IVs. Satellite is based on ThunderStorm library. Satellite is under MIT license.
